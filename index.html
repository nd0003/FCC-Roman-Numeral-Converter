nd03's Decimal to Roman Numeral Converter

This is my Roman Numeral Converter that I wrote in JavaScript for freeCodeCamp.org. You can use the converter by doing the following:

  • 1. Press F12 on your keyboard. This will bring up the devtools for your browser. You can also open devtools by right clicking anywhere on the page, then clicking "Inspect."
  • 2. At the top of the devtools, you will see a navigation bar. Click on "console" to bring up the JavaScript console.
  • 3. Once you are in the console, you can use the following function to convert a number of your choice into the Roman numeral form:
    convertToRoman(number)
    (You put the number of your choice in the brackets.)
    Doing this will convert any decimal number into its Roman numeral form!
